Time Frame Characteristics

A. The time frame for medicine online sales is concentrated between 8:00am to 21:00pm. Different parts of China see different sales.

Dingdang medicine concerns netizens’ medicine demand at all time and finds that in 24 hours a day, the time frame between 8:00am to 21:00pm sees a concentrated sale. The sales peak time in the day time is at 10:00am while 19:00pm reaches another smaller peak, which is in consistent with our netizens’ routine habit for all shopping made online. The top-selling cold medicines and medicines for respiratory system see its peak of the day between 9:00am and 11:00am. And at the night time when people’s minds are full of “romantic fantasies”, contraceptive products exhibit a prominent demand.

B. Medicines for immunity adjustment win a universal attention. Sales in the middle and west part of China is higher than the east.

Thanks for the whole nation’s great concern about how to strengthen the body’s immunity, relevant medicines are sold well across the entire country. To be more specific, sales in the middle and west part of China is higher than the east, especially between 9:00am and 21:00pm, this medicine category almost stays in the top 3 sales all the time in the middle and west part of China.

C. Medicines for sphagitis and trachitis in the east show obvious higher sales than in the middle and west.

A significantly larger number of people in the east are buying medicines for sphagitis and trachitis in the east than in the middle and west. Only in a very few time frames that the sales of this medicine category could be listed in the top 10 in the middle and west part of China. While in the east, except mid night between 23:00pm and 5:00am, this medicine category remains its steady position on the top 10 sales list in other time frames of the day. Expectorants and cough suppressants see high sales across China and they are sold better in the east than in the middle and west, which means people living in the east have to enhance their awareness of throat protection.

Wide regional difference is present in medicine online market. Netizens of different provinces have different preferences.

Across the country as a whole, people in every province attach major concerns to their sexual and health care demands, since along with the improvement of Chinese people’s overall living standards, people’s consciousness to sex and health care is also significantly enhanced. And due to Chinese people’s restrained and introverted personalities, they feel shy to buy sexual safety products in pharmacies. Therefore, online shopping becomes a preferred way to buy such products. Sexual medicine Viagra Sildenafil Citrate Tablets and health care product Renhe oral solution for nourishing blood and benefiting vital energy are listed on the top 10 list in almost every province.

A. Heilongjiang, Inner Mongolia and Henan provinces are under high pressures.

Cardiac cerebrovascular medications for hyperlipidemia, hypertension and arteriosclerosis are ranked on the top of the sales list in Heilongjiang, Inner Mongolia and Henan provinces. Therefore, Dingdang medicine kindly reminds people living in these provinces be careful about your Cardiac cerebrovascular health conditions.

B. Beijing, Hebei and Ningxia province have the sales of masks on the top 10 list

In order to reduce annoyances from severe smog issues that are widely condemned in capital city of Beijing and Hebei province, masks become indispensible in people’s daily life, which ranks them on the top list in the two regions. The reason why masks are sold well in Ningxia province is due to the geographical environment in the province with a lot of windy and dusty weathers.

C. People in Shaanxi province imply a strong will to have children.

People living in northwest China’s Shaanxi province imply a strong will to have children because Dingdang medicine finds that medicines for prompting pregnancy, preventing miscarriages, regulating menstruation and nourishing blood top the list while in most other provinces, sales of contraceptive products such as condoms and contraceptive pills stay an all-time high.

D. Tibet people have to use more gynecological medicines.

The top 3 best-selling medicines in Tibet are all gynecological medicines, due to the relatively low medical levels in the vast pasturing areas of Tibet and poor hygienic and health habits of the people there. Hence, Dingdang medicine wishes an immediate improvement of the local medical and hygienic conditions.

There is a significant difference between north and south China’s medication tendency; people in the north are busy with anti-smog while people in the south stress more about health care.

Dingdang medicine finds that different natural environment and humanity factors between north and south China lead to significant difference in meditation selections. Jintoo 3D protection mask is seen in north China’s top 10 list, while in south China’s list, there is no mask at all. It is obvious that air quality in the north is worse than that of south. Two health care products are listed in south China’s list, namely By-health calcium softgems and Renhe oral solution for nourishing blood and benefiting vital energy, while health care products are absent in north China’s list, which means people in the south stress more about their health care than people in the north.
